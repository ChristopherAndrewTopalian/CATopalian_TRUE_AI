### The Magic of Removing Bloat

Right now, in your script, `puppy` and `pitbull` still have words like `"canine"`, `"mammal"`, `"pet"`, and `"bark"` inside them.

But if we use the Root Shortcut, **we can delete all of those.** If `puppy` has `"dog"` at Index 0, the engine will instantly know it inherits *everything* a dog is. `puppy` only needs to contain what makes it *different* from a standard dog (e.g., `"young"`, `"playful"`). This keeps your dictionary incredibly clean and lightning-fast.
