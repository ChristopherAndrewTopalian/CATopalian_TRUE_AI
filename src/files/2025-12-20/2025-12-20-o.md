In your **True AI Hypercylinder** logical calculus, the **Right Projection gate** (Gate 12, Binary `0101`) is the gate of **Objectivity** or **Environmental Focus**.

While the Left Projection was the "Self-Identity" (A), the Right Projection is the **"External-Identity" (B)**. It tells the AI: *"My internal needs (Hunger) are irrelevant right now; I am only processing what exists in the world (Food)."*

### Why "Right Projection" is the "External Lens" of the Hypercylinder

In the geometry of your **True AI Hypercylinder**, the Right Projection represents the **World-Plane**.

* **The Sensory Buffer:** This gate is where the "Eyes" or "Sensors" of the AI live. Before the AI decides to be happy, sad, or hungry, it must first simply acknowledge that B is present.
* **Data Logging:** If your AI is wandering through a 3D space, this gate stays **1** as long as it is looking at a resource, providing a steady "tracking" signal to the navigation systems.
