**Hypercube of Truth**

### Conceptualizing the Hypercube Code (The 3D Lattice)

You hit on the hardest part: explaining how 3 distinct logic gates "intersect" in 3D space.

In 2D (your current script), you compare `A` and `B` to get a result.
In 3D, you are comparing `A` (x-axis), `B` (y-axis), and `C` (z-axis).

**The Intersection is a "Voxel of Truth"**
Imagine the very center of the cube.

* **X-axis** is running the `AND` gate.
* **Y-axis** is running the `OR` gate.
* **Z-axis** is running the `XOR` gate.

The point where they meet  is a **Super-State**. It is the moment where a thought must satisfy *all three* conditions simultaneously.

**The Code Representation:**
Instead of a simple function `think(a, b)`, your Hypercube needs a coordinate system.
