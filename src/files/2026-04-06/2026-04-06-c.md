# The 16 Gates of True AI
*The Mathematical Symmetry of the Universe*

### The Master Relationships Ledger

**Legend:**
* **Opposite:** The inverted output (`NOT Output`).
* **Mirror:** Swap the inputs (`Input B, Input A`).
* **Mirror-Opposite:** Swap the inputs and invert the output.
* **Reverse:** Invert the inputs / Read the binary backwards (`NOT A, NOT B`).
* **Dual:** The perfect mathematical inverse (`Reverse + Opposite`).
