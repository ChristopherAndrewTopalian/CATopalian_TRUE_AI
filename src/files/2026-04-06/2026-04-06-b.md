*The Mathematical Symmetry of the Universe*

### The Master Relationships Ledger

**Legend:**
* **Opposite:** The inverted output.
* **Mirror:** The output if you swap Input A and Input B.
* **Mirror-Opposite:** The output if you swap the inputs AND invert them.
* **Dual:** The perfect mathematical inverse of the gate.
