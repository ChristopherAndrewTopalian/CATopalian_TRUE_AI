The XNOR gate (Exclusive NOR) is the gate of **Resonance and Symbiosis.**

To comprehend why this is the case, we have to look at its mathematical law: XNOR only returns a `1` if both inputs are exactly the same. If input A and input B are both `1`, it returns a `1`.
