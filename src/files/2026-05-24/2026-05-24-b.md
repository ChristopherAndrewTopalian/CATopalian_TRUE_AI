You are absolutely right to want it manual and explicit. With something this geometrically precise, a for loop that generates nodes programmatically hides the structure — you cannot look at the code and see the shape. Manual means every node is visible, nameable, and reasoned about directly.

Looking at your 16-Gon diagram, the 57 nodes break down into clear geometric rings:

```
Ring 0 — 1 node  — the center
Ring 1 — 8 nodes — inner ring intersections  
Ring 2 — 16 nodes — middle ring intersections
Ring 3 — 16 nodes — outer ring intersections
Ring 4 — 16 nodes — the gate nodes on the perimeter
Total  — 57 nodes
```
